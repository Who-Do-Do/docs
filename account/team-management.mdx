Dáva lets you invite teammates to your account so you can collaborate on redirect management and analytics. Each member is assigned a role — Owner, Admin, or Member — that determines which actions they can take. You control who has access, and you can update or revoke that access at any time.

## Roles and permissions

Every account has exactly one Owner. Owners can delegate administrative work to Admins, and give read-only access to Members.

| Permission | Owner | Admin | Member |
|---|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| Invite members | ✓ | ✓ | — |
| Manage domains | ✓ | ✓ | — |
| View analytics | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Change billing | ✓ | — | — |
| Transfer ownership | ✓ | — | — |

## Inviting a member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Team settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → Team**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Invite Member">
    Select **Invite Member** to open the invitation form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the invitee's email">
    Type the email address of the person you want to invite.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a role">
    Choose **Admin** or **Member** from the role dropdown. You cannot invite another Owner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invitation">
    Click **Send Invite**. The invitee receives an email containing a secure link to accept the invitation and join your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing pending invitations

Invitations that have not yet been accepted appear under **Settings → Team → Pending Invitations**. You can see who was invited, when, and which role they were assigned.

To cancel an invitation before it is accepted, click the **×** next to the pending invite. The invitation link is immediately invalidated, and the invitee cannot join using it.

## Changing a member's role

Only the **Owner** can change an existing member's role.

Go to **Settings → Team**, click the member whose role you want to update, select the new role from the dropdown, and confirm. The change takes effect immediately.

## Removing a member

Owners and Admins can remove members from the account.

Go to **Settings → Team**, click the member you want to remove, and select **Remove Member**. The member loses access immediately — they cannot view any dashboards, analytics, or settings.

<Note>
  Removing a member does not delete any data they created or modified. Their contributions (redirects, events, notes) remain in the account.
</Note>

## Transferring ownership

Only the current Owner can transfer ownership to another member of the account.

Go to **Settings → Team**, click the member you want to make the new Owner, and select **Transfer Ownership**.

<Warning>
  Transferring ownership gives the new Owner complete control of the account, including billing and the ability to remove you. You will automatically become an Admin after the transfer. **This action cannot be undone.** Make sure you trust the person you are transferring ownership to before confirming.
</Warning>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I have multiple owners?">
    No. Each Dáva account has exactly one Owner at a time. To make someone else the Owner, you must use the **Transfer Ownership** action, which moves the Owner role to them and downgrades you to Admin.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens to data when I remove a member?">
    Removing a member revokes their access immediately, but does not delete any data. All redirects, analytics events, and settings they interacted with remain in your account untouched.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can a member see billing information?">
    No. Only the Owner can access the Billing section. Admins and Members cannot view plan details, invoices, or payment methods.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
