Dáva offers a free 14-day trial so you can explore the full product before committing. After the trial, you can subscribe to a Pro plan billed monthly or annually through Stripe. Manage your subscription, update your payment method, and download invoices directly from the Billing portal inside your account settings.

## Accessing the Billing page

Go to **Settings → Billing**. This page shows your current plan, the next billing date, and your usage versus the plan limits for domains and event volume.

Only the account **Owner** can access the Billing page. Admins and Members do not see this section.

## Upgrading to Pro

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Billing settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → Billing** and click **Upgrade**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Stripe Checkout">
    You are redirected to Stripe Checkout. Select monthly or annual billing, then enter your payment method.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your subscription">
    Click **Subscribe**. Your Pro subscription activates immediately and your account limits are updated in real time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing your subscription

Click **Manage Subscription** on the Billing page to open the **Stripe Customer Portal**. From there you can:

- **Update your payment method** — replace or add a card without cancelling your subscription
- **Download invoices** — access PDF invoices for every billing period under the **Invoices** section
- **Cancel your subscription** — stop future charges; see the Cancellation section below for what happens next

## Usage limits

<Note>
  Some features — including the number of domains you can add and your monthly event volume — are subject to plan limits. The Billing page shows your current usage alongside each limit. If you approach a limit, a warning banner appears in the dashboard. Upgrade to Pro (or contact support for high-volume needs) to raise your limits.
</Note>

## Cancellation

You can cancel at any time from the Stripe Customer Portal.

Cancelling stops future charges. Your account stays fully active until the end of the current billing period, after which it is downgraded. Existing data is preserved — nothing is deleted when you cancel.

<Tip>
  If you cancel and change your mind, you can resubscribe at any time from **Settings → Billing** before or after the period ends.
</Tip>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What happens when my trial ends?">
    When the 14-day trial expires, your account is downgraded and some features may become unavailable. Your existing data — redirects, analytics, team members — is preserved. Upgrade to a Pro plan to restore full access without losing anything.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Contact support to discuss a refund. Dáva processes payments through Stripe, so any approved refund is returned to your original payment method. Reach out as soon as possible after a charge you want to dispute.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I get an invoice for my company?">
    Open the **Stripe Customer Portal** from **Settings → Billing → Manage Subscription**. Under **Invoices**, you can view and download a PDF for each billing period. If you need a custom invoice with your company's VAT number or address, update your billing details in the portal and the next invoice will reflect the new information.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
