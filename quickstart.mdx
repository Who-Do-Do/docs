---

This guide walks you through everything you need to go from a blank Dáva account to a live, fully tracked campaign link. By the end you'll have a verified subdomain, a configured redirect mode, and a URL ready to drop into your first campaign.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [app.davazmysel.com](https://app.davazmysel.com) and click **Sign Up**. Enter your email address and choose a password. Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link inside. Once verified, you'll land directly in the Dáva dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your first domain">
    In the dashboard, navigate to **Domains → Add Domain**. Fill in two fields:

    - **Subdomain** — the full subdomain you want to track through, e.g. `go.yourbrand.com`
    - **Destination host** — where traffic should ultimately land, e.g. `https://yourbrand.com`

    Click **Create Domain**. Dáva will display the CNAME record you need to add in the next step. Your domain status will show as **Pending** until the DNS record is detected.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your CNAME record">
    Log in to your DNS provider and add a CNAME record pointing your subdomain to `client.davazmysel.com`. The exact steps vary by provider:

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Cloudflare">
        1. Open your domain in the Cloudflare dashboard and go to **DNS → Records**.
        2. Click **+ Add record**.
        3. Set **Type** to `CNAME`.
        4. Set **Name** to your subdomain prefix — e.g. `go` (Cloudflare appends your zone automatically).
        5. Set **Target** to `client.davazmysel.com`.
        6. Set **Proxy status** to **DNS only** (grey cloud). This is required — see the warning below.
        7. Leave **TTL** on **Auto** and click **Save**.

        <Warning>
          You must disable Cloudflare's orange-cloud proxy for this record. Set it to **DNS only** (grey cloud). If the proxy is enabled, Cloudflare terminates the connection before it reaches Dáva, and TLS provisioning will fail.
        </Warning>
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Route 53">
        1. Open the **Route 53** console and go to **Hosted zones → your domain**.
        2. Click **Create record**.
        3. Set **Record name** to your full subdomain, e.g. `go.yourbrand.com`.
        4. Set **Record type** to `CNAME`.
        5. Set **Value** to `client.davazmysel.com`.
        6. Set **TTL** to `300`.
        7. Click **Create records**.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="GoDaddy">
        1. Go to your domain's **DNS settings** in GoDaddy.
        2. Click **Add** and select **CNAME** as the record type.
        3. Set **Name** to your subdomain prefix, e.g. `go`.
        4. Set **Value** to `client.davazmysel.com`.
        5. Set **TTL** to **1 hour**.
        6. Click **Save**.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

    <Note>
      DNS propagation typically takes **1–15 minutes**, though some registrars can take longer. Dáva verifies your CNAME across four independent public resolvers — Cloudflare, Google, Quad9, and OpenDNS — before marking your domain **Active**. You can watch the status update live in the dashboard, or click **Retry Verification** to trigger an immediate check.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a redirect mode">
    Once your domain is **Active**, go to **Domain Settings → Mode** and choose one of the four operating modes:

    | Mode | Best for | Setup time |
    |------|----------|------------|
    | **A — Simple Redirect** | Host-swap with UTM passthrough | ~2 min |
    | **B — Structured Passthrough** | URL-convention attribution (e.g. `/ref/{brand}/{campaign}`) | ~10 min |
    | **C — Manual Mappings** | Explicit per-URL routing table | ~10 min |
    | **D — Hosted Pages** | Static landing pages with injected tracking | ~15 min |

    <Tip>
      If you're unsure where to start, choose **Mode A — Simple Redirect**. It's the fastest to configure and works immediately with any UTM-tagged URL. You can switch modes later if your needs change.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Build your first tracked URL">
    With your domain active and mode configured, construct your campaign URL by appending UTM parameters to your subdomain:

    ```
    https://go.yourbrand.com/?utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=paid&utm_campaign=spring-sale
    ```

    Dáva captures every parameter on the inbound hit and stores it alongside the visit record. You can build URLs manually or use the **URL Builder** tool in the dashboard (go to **Domains → your domain → URL Builder**) to generate properly encoded links without typing parameters by hand.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Before sending any link to a live audience, use the **Test URL** tool to confirm your redirect is working as expected. Go to **Domains → your domain → Test URL**, enter a sample link, and Dáva shows you exactly where the visitor will land and which attribution fields will be recorded.
</Note>
