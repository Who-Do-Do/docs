Dáva is the redirect management and conversion tracking platform built for marketers who need to know exactly which traffic source is driving results. You CNAME a subdomain to Dáva, choose how you want traffic routed, and every click that passes through is captured with full UTM attribution, device data, and geo context — no code changes on your destination site required. Whether you're running paid ads, email campaigns, or affiliate programs, Dáva gives you one place to manage your links and measure what's working.

## How onboarding works

From sign-up to your first live tracked link, there are four steps. Most teams complete the whole flow in under ten minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [app.davazmysel.com](https://app.davazmysel.com) and sign up with your email. Verify your address, and you're in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a domain and point your CNAME">
    In the dashboard, go to **Domains → Add Domain**. Enter a subdomain (e.g. `go.yourbrand.com`) and a destination host. Then add a CNAME record in your DNS provider pointing your subdomain to `client.davazmysel.com`. Dáva verifies propagation automatically across four public resolvers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a redirect mode">
    Choose how Dáva should route traffic through your subdomain — Simple Redirect for straightforward host-swaps, Structured Passthrough for URL-convention attribution, Manual Mappings for per-URL control, or Hosted Pages for static landing pages with built-in tracking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send your first campaign">
    Build a tracked URL using your verified subdomain and UTM parameters, drop it into your campaign, and watch attributed visits arrive in real time on your analytics dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
